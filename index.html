Bio

Ramiro Huizar, who also goes by "Nous", is a self-taught artist who was raised on the south-westside chicago. Being raised in a mostly white neigborhood, Ramiro begin to seek exposure to public artworks. This lead him to venture closer to the city, areas like Pilsen, hyde-park, logan-square and little village. These neigborhoods are rich strong holds for Mexican-American Culture.
